Go global with ITCQF

(Photo by ITCQF)

 

Software testers can get certified with ITCQB, so do tech writers with ITCQF, International Technical Communication Qualifications Foundation. This certificate can increase the employment opportunities on the job market worldwide.

The Vistula studies curriculum includes practical preparation for the ITCQF Foundation Level certificate. It means that the Tech Comm graduates can take the exam after solid training. Integrating ITCQF preparation into postgraduate studies is a real opportunity to learn a lot in a short period of time.
