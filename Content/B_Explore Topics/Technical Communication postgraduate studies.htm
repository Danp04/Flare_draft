Technical Communication postgraduate studies

For those interested in entering the profession, the first thing that draws attention is the lack of full-time courses available for tech writers. That's why Dariusz Drezno decided to meet the business demand for content specialists.

Attempts to start a postgraduate university course have been made several times in the past few years, but without success. However, in 2020 the team didn't give up even despite the ongoing pandemic. For the first time tech comm studies has become available online at the Vistula University in Warsaw.

A group of students from all over Poland has formed to acquire skills that will help in getting a job as a tech writer. The experts from the tech comm industry in Poland took the role of lecturers. Thanks to the support of partners, the students have the opportunity to use specialized tools used in the design and publication of technical content.

The partners include:

  • Techwriter.pl

  • DREDAR

  • Madcap Software

  • Atlassian

  • ITCQF

  • TechSmith

  • Syncro Soft

  • MemoQ

It is challenging to open a new field of study. Cooperation with the university means :

  • fulfilling legal and educational requirements

  • preparing the syllabus

  • precized objectives

  • predetermined schedule

As you can notice, studies or courses offering full preparation for the profession are very promising for the future.
