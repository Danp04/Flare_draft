Techwriter.pl as the cradle of the Polish tech comm scene

In Poland, there has long been a portal for IT software testers, Testerzy.pl. However, there wasn’t any source of information for people involved in the broadly defined technical communications industry.

In 2013, Dariusz Drezno, a Polish software testing expert decided to set a portal for the Polish tech writers, Techwriter.pl. With its ups and downs, it's now the most reliable source of knowledge for the local industry professionals and enthusiasts. Apart from information about Poland and the world, it provides:

  • job offers

  • interviews

  • lots of training materials

  • interesting articles

  • salary surveys

For more details about the origins of Techwriter.pl and its contributors read the article by Tom Johnson: The Untold Story of Techwriter.pl.
