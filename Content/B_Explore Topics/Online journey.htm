Online activities

With the development of technology, many things happen online. Although you can associate the online environment mostly with written activities, today’s reality has revealed the potential of live online events and remote work. High interaction of people on the web encourages the formation of industry sub-groups. The Polish tech comm passionates have also gone viral.

Tech Writer codes?

A recently created polish initiative is a podcast, Tech Writer koduje [eng, Tech Writer codes]. It’s in the Polish native language and addresses aspects that make the work of tech writers easier. Podcasting is very convenient as you can do various things while listening to it.

The online groups can be your starting point in a community development. It’s costs-effective and easy to organize. However, it's more difficult to keep members active. The examples presented above illustrate that many activities are possible when committed enthusiasts team up.
