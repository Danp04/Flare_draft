Getting Started

In this guide, you will discover the story behind the successful creation of the tech comm community in Poland. Following the path, you will find out how much can be done, not necessarily with a predefined budget to bring the industry together.

Who this guide is for

This guide is a roadmap for you: tech writers, documentation developers, how to engage people with a passion for technology and content to collaborate as a community. You're sure to find a starting point and source of inspiration here.

How to navigate through this guide

It's best to start either with Explore Poland section or the video meeting at the homepage to find out what your Polish colleagues have done to make the community visible. Learn about the Tech comm training and find out more about the industry events.

Then, you can follow the Techwriting community building guidelines section that provides a path on what to start with and how to keep going.

Finally, check the FAQ section, where you can find answers to possible problems a new community can face.
