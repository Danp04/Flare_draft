Surprisingly, the term community originates from the French language. It is a combination of the words together and gift. It's an integral part of human existence. People have always formed groups or sub-groups through different situations, location, common interest, or practice. The focus of this guide is to the community of practice.

Many communities of practice operate in various domains. People need a collaborative space where they can interact, look for information, exchange experiences. The community of tech writers as well. Scroll down to explore how it all started in case of the Polish practicioners.
