Techwriting community

 

Surprisingly, the term community is a combination of the words together and gift. It originates from the French language.

People have always formed groups or sub-groups through different situations, location, common interest, or practice. The focus of this guide is on the community of practice. Many communities of practice operate in various domains. People need a collaborative space where they can interact, look for information, and exchange experiences.
